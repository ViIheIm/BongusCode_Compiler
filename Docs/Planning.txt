⫸ Call-site inlining:
Research the idea of asking the compiler to inline a function, but at the callsite.
Example:

Nihil Add(i32 a, i32 b)
{
  Claudere a + b.
}

i32 main(Nihil)
{
  i32 a = inlineplz? Add(3, 4).

  Claudere a.
}
